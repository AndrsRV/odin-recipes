Arepas Recipe!

Arepas depending on who you ask are from Venezuela or Colombia

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of pre-cooked cornmeal (Harina PAN)
  • 2 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of oil or butter (optional)

Intructions

  1. In a large bowl add water and the precooked cornmeal (Harina PAN)
  2. Add salt and stir untill you get a concise not soft neither hard body
  3. Usually arepas are rounded but can make them whatever shape you like
  4. Heat the cooking pan medium heat
  5. Add a little oil or butter to the pan
  6. Place the arepas on the pan and cook for about 5-7
