Arroz Chino Recipe!

The venezuelan arroz chino comes from chinese inmigrant that went to country

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Chicken
  • Shrimp
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Eggs

Instructions

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Season and cook chicken as you like it
  3. Cook the shrimp (make sure is cooked)
  4. Add everything all together
  5. Stir and while cooking add soy sauce to coloring the rice

ENJOY YOUR MEAL!
