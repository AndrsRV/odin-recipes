Brownies Recipe!

Brownies are a really good dessert and if you like chocolate you are on the right track!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate chips
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Cacao

Instructions

  1. Melt the chocolate with the butter
  2. Mix wet and dry ingredients in different bowls
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  4. Pour everything on an 8x8 pan lined with parchment paper
  5. Finally bake at 325 degress for 40-45 mins

ENJOY YOUR MEAL!
